Data Deletion Request

Your data, your rights. Here's how to request deletion of your personal information.

1. Your Right to Data Deletion

Under Mexico's Nueva Ley Federal de Proteccion de Datos Personales en Posesion de los Particulares (NLFPDPPP), you have the right to request the cancellation (deletion) of your personal data held by Bravo Ad Agency. This is part of your ARCO rights (Access, Rectification, Cancellation, and Opposition).

We are committed to honoring your data deletion requests promptly and transparently.

2. What Data We Collect

Through our PowerISA recruiting campaigns and META applications, we may collect the following personal data:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Professional information: Resume details, work experience, qualifications.
  • Platform data: Information provided through META lead forms (Facebook, Instagram).
  • Communication records: Messages and correspondence related to recruiting.

For full details on our data practices, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

3. How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your personal data, you must submit a written request via mail to the following address:

Bravo Ad Agency
Attn: Daniela F.
Constitucion 64, Colonia Centro
Uruapan, Michoacan De Ocampo,CP. 60000. Mexico

Your request must include:

  • Your full legal name.
  • A clear description of the personal data you wish to have deleted.
  • A copy of an official identification document (e.g., INE, passport) to verify your identity.
  • A return mailing address or contact method where we can send confirmation.
  • Your signature.

4. Deletion Process

Once we receive your written request, the following process will be followed:

  1. Receipt and Acknowledgment We will acknowledge receipt of your request within 5 business days of receiving your letter.
  2. Identity Verification We will verify your identity using the documentation provided. If additional information is needed, we will contact you at the return address specified in your request.
  3. Data Review We will identify all personal data associated with your request across our systems, including SaaS applications and cloud services.
  4. Deletion Execution Your personal data will be securely deleted using appropriate methods, including overwriting, crypto-shredding, or secure deletion tools, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
  5. Confirmation You will receive written confirmation that your data has been deleted, sent to the return address provided in your request.

5. Processing Timeline

In accordance with the NLFPDPPP and our commitment to ARCO rights, your request will be processed within the following timeframe:

5
Business days to acknowledge receipt
15
Business days maximum to complete deletion
5
Business days to send written confirmation

6. Exceptions

In certain circumstances, we may be unable to fully delete your data, including but not limited to:

  • Legal obligations: When retention is required by applicable law or regulation.
  • Active legal holds: When data is subject to ongoing litigation or investigation.
  • Contractual obligations: When data must be retained to fulfill existing contractual commitments.

If any exception applies, we will notify you in writing with a clear explanation of the reason and the expected duration of continued retention.

7. After Deletion

Once your data has been deleted:

  • Your personal information will be permanently removed from our active systems.
  • Backup copies will be purged in accordance with our backup rotation schedule, as described in our Privacy Policy.
  • We will no longer be able to contact you regarding recruiting campaigns or opportunities.
  • A record of the deletion request and confirmation will be retained for audit and compliance purposes only.

8. Questions

If you have questions about the data deletion process or your ARCO rights, please send your inquiry in writing to the address listed above, addressed to Daniela Flores.

For more information about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
